Our Menu

Beshbarmaq

If, when visiting Kazakhstan for the first time, you ask what Kazakh dishes to try, you will definitely be answered: “Besbarmak.” The second name for besbarmak is et (“meat in Kazakh”). The dish consists of boiled meat (horse meat, lamb, beef or camel meat), thin dough and broth.

Cost: 3490

Quyrdaq

Kuyrdak (translated as “frying” - named because of the cooking method) is the national Kazakh roast. It is prepared only from fresh meat. To prepare kuyrdak, finely chopped fatty lamb, tripe, intestines, lungs and liver are fried in its own juice, sometimes with the addition of sour cream.

Cost: 3290

Sirne

The young lamb simmers on the fire for a long time - this is how the meat becomes very tender. Onions, potatoes and other vegetables are added to the roast (depending on the region). Syrne is prepared in a large cauldron to serve to all neighbors.

Cost: 2990

Bauyrsaq

Baursaks are pieces of yeast dough fried in a large amount of oil. Baursaks taste sweetish and can be served as a separate dish. But, as a rule, Baursaks always eat with something. A good housewife will serve them not only with the main course, but also with tea.

Cost: 790

Zhent

Popular dishes in Kazakhstan are not only meat and dairy. Zhent is a dessert made from crushed cottage cheese, millet, sugar, raisins and butter. After mixing the ingredients, the dessert can be given any shape. When the zhent has hardened, it is cut and served for tea.

Cost: 790

Irimshik

This is cottage cheese that has a slightly sweet taste even without added sugar. The milk is brought to a boil over low heat, then cooled slightly, the starter is added, stirred and left for a couple of hours. Then put the milk back on low heat and bring to a boil. Then put the cottage cheese in a bag and allow the excess liquid to drain.

Cost: 890

Qurt

We can say that kurt is a dried concentrate of natural milk with a natural preservative - salt, so kurt is a way of preserving milk. Kurt was invented by the nomadic peoples of Central Asia.

Cost: 290

Qymyz

Qymyz is a fermented milk drink made from mare's milk, obtained as a result of lactic and alcoholic fermentation using Bulgarian and acidophilic lactic acid rods and yeast. The drink is foamy, whitish in color, and tastes sweet and sour.

Cost: 1290

Shubat

Shubat is a fermented milk drink made from camel milk. Compared to koumiss, it has a higher fat content. Depending on the aging, there are several types of shubat - light, medium strength and the strongest.

Cost: 1190
